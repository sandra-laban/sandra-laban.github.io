Learning Plan

Bootcamp learning plan

28 September 2023

The long term goal

My plan is to level up my technical skills and become a Front-End Developer specialist. My goal is to become a senior in this field, work with product teams on bigger projects and move from corporate websites.

Strengths and limitations at Bootcamp

I would say that I can rush through things and focus more on efficiency instead of quality. As well as I can go to other end and get to perfectionist mode. Also, the technical part of the learning may be the biggest challenge as I'm not always confident in my abilities to grasp complex concepts and navigate through new systems.

Non-technical challenge at Bootcamp

The non-technical challenge will be to stay on top of the material and not rush through things. Collaborating on group projects may also be challenging because it will be online, so communication could be slower.

Human skills I would like to develop at Bootcamp

Definitely to develop more efficient communication, as well as giving and receiving constructive feedback.

Expectations from the Bootcamp team

I hope the bootcamp team can provide help, guidance, engagement and a friendly atmosphere as I learn. I want to gain more confidence and knowledge, so I can reach my full potential during this journey.

Expectations of myself on Bootcamp

If I get stuck with finding solution by my self I will reach out to peers or bootcamp team. I aim to be fully engaged with the bootcamp by following rules, being on time, being prepared, and completing objectives on time.


Foundations learning plan

9 September 2023

The long term goal

Well, so far I was working as web developer/designer in the marketing team, and it can be a bit challenging at times. I often find myself stuck on technical stuff, and there aren't many colleagues around who can help me out. So, my plan is to level up my technical skills and become a Front-End Developer specialist. I want to be part of a bigger team with more developers where we can all learn from each other. My dream down the road is to become a senior in this field. And, looking even further ahead, I see myself starting a company with my husband - a startup adventure.

Strengths and limitations

Strengths: Whether it's troubleshooting project issues, handling real-life challenges, or strategizing during a game like chess, I'm all in. Problem-solving is something I truly enjoy.

Also,I'm one persistent cookie. When I decide to learn something, I don't give up easily. That determination of mine keeps me going, even when things get tough. So, count on me to stick with it and see things through.

Limitations: Sometimes, it's tough for me to ask for help or step out of my comfort zone. I tend to overthink things, and I have this perfectionist streak in me.

When it comes to complex problems, it takes me a little extra time to really wrap my head around them. I might need a few more explanations to fully understand things. But here's the upside: once I do grasp it, I'm all in and ready to tackle the challenge effectively. That's just how I roll!

Core human skills

Besides all the technical stuff, I'm really looking to grow in some core human skills. One biggie for me is better communication. I'm kind of an introvert, so socialising and expressing myself clearly can be a bit of a challenge. I want to get better at connecting with others and making my ideas heard. So, improving my communication and interaction skills is high on my list while I'm at Dev Academy.

Managing workload

I'm going to work for about 8 hours every day. I'll take a nice one-hour break for lunch. And in between, I'll have short breaks to make some coffee and stretch my legs. That way, I can stay productive without feeling too tired.I'll keep my evenings free to spend time with my kids and husband. If I need to, I can stay up late to catch up on work.

In order to maintain a positive and stress-free mindset, I'll take little breaks from the computer when I need to. Maybe go for a walk or have a chat with a friend. I'll stick with my yoga practice because it really helps me feel relaxed and centered. So, staying positive and stress-free should be doable.

Seeking help commitment

I will ask for help from my cohort members if I can't resolve an issue on my own within 30 minutes. I'll give it a good try, but if I'm still stuck after that time, I will try my best not to hesitate to reach out to the Dev Academy facilitators for assistance. I won't stay stuck for too long and will keep the learning process moving smoothly. We're in this together, right?

Expectations from the Facilitation team

What I'm really hoping for from the Facilitation team is that they'll be patient and understanding. I mean, if I need something explained, even if it takes them repeating it five times or breaking it down like they're explaining to a five-year-old, that's perfectly fine with me. Basically, I just want them to be there to help me understand and not feel shy about asking for clarification.

Commitments

I'm fortunate to have wonderful support from my husband, who takes care of our children's activities and maintains our home seamlessly. This support allows me to fully concentrate on my course; however, there may be occasions when I'll need to step in for school pick-ups or drop-offs. I've planned to set aside dedicated time for my yoga practice in the evenings and quality time with family and friends on weekends.
